White Rapids Rafting

At White Rapids Rafting, our mission is to inspire adventure and connection through unforgettable white-water experiences. We’re dedicated to safety, sustainability, and the thrill of the outdoors, helping every guest paddle beyond their limits and create lasting memories on the river.

History

White Rapids Rafting was born from a love of wild rivers and good adventure. What started as a few friends chasing rapids turned into a full-blown passion for sharing the thrill of white-water with others. For over two decades,

we’ve been guiding paddlers through roaring rapids, sunlit canyons, and some of nature’s most breathtaking places — all while keeping safety, laughter, and the spirit of adventure at the heart of every trip.
